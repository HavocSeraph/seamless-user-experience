Phase 6 Test Scenarios 
1. Redis Pub/Sub Architecture 
• CRITICAL: Verify TWO separate ioredis instances exist (one for pub, one for sub) 
• Verify: The same Redis client is NOT used for both publishing and subscribing 
• Verify: Sub client calls client.subscribe() on initialization 
• Verify: Pub client calls client.publish() to emit events 
• RED FLAG: Single Redis client used for both pub and sub = deadlock risk — ioredis throws when 
you call subscribe() on a client that has published
